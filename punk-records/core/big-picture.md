### ElimuAfrica — The Product
What's being built. Federated three-server architecture, real ML (MCTS, Bayesian tracking), 45+ student pages, teacher tools, admin dashboards. Version 1.6.7 and growing daily. This is where the time and energy go.

### Narrative Engine — The Voice
Builds an audience of believers on LinkedIn before the product launches. Vegapunk architecture: multiple AI personas sharing one brain, expressing the education crisis story in different voices. The pre-launch strategy playing out in phases.

### Punk Records MCP — The Memory
Central knowledge system that makes every Claude Code session smarter than the last. Second implementation of the Vegapunk theory. Stores identity, brand knowledge, patterns, decisions, lessons. The brain that holds everything together.

### Elimu Community Light — The Community
The NGO side. Community development: ECD, education access, livelihoods. Brand identity designed through Pulse. Connected to ElimuAfrica by shared mission, different execution.

### The Freelance Work
Independent brand strategy and design. The craft that pays for the cathedral. Started as Pulse Brand Studio (agency), evolved to honest freelance (imaslam) when the agency model felt misleading for a solo practitioner.

### The Client Portfolio
Five completed brand identities, each proving the methodology works across completely different industries — car rental, fashion, fitness, NGO, tech retail. Each brand has different visual output but the same underlying process.

### The Methodology
Nine-phase brand identity process. Discovery meeting → Research → Proposal → Alignment → Strategy → Strategy review → Visuals → Visual review → Applications & handoff. Strategy drives everything. Visuals are derived, never arbitrary.

## How It All Connects

**The Vegapunk thread:** Narrative Engine and Punk Records MCP are two implementations of the same theory — central knowledge, specialized agents, human in the loop. The theory will keep evolving.

**The brand thread:** Every client brand built through the freelance work deepens the methodology. The methodology informs how ElimuAfrica's brand will eventually be designed (strategy first, visuals derived). The content creation approach mirrors the brand philosophy (authenticity, value, customer as hero).

**The mission thread:** ElimuAfrica is the destination. Everything else is either building toward it (Narrative Engine building audience, Punk Records building memory), funding it (freelance work), connected to it (Elimu Community Light), or was the path that led to it (Apple Empire year, quitting the comfort zone).

**The personal thread:** The curiosity, the stubbornness about quality, the relentless pursuit, the refusal to settle — these show up in everything. In the brand work (terminate uncommitted clients), in the code (strict TypeScript, zero violations), in the content (value over noise), in the product (real ML, not shortcuts).

---

## Where It's Heading

The immediate focus: build ElimuAfrica. Ship the product. Reach real students.

The larger vision is still forming — the Vegapunk theory, the distributed consciousness idea, the concept of multiple specialized agents sharing one growing brain. Punk Records and the Narrative Engine are early experiments. Where this goes depends on what gets learned by building.

The craft (freelance brand work) and the hobby (content creation) continue alongside. They're not distractions from the mission — they're what keeps the skills sharp and the bills paid while the cathedral gets built.
