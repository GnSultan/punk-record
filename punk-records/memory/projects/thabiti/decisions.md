# Thabiti — Decision Log

### 2024-03-15 — StoryBrand framework for positioning

**Decision:** Use the StoryBrand framework to position Thabiti as a guide, not a hero.

**Reasoning:** The traveler is the hero — they're the one on the journey. Thabiti is the reliable companion that removes the worry of unreliable transport. This shifts messaging from "we have great cars" to "you'll never be stranded."

**Tags:** brand, storybrand, positioning

---

### 2024-04-01 — Reliability as emotional positioning, not feature

**Decision:** Position reliability as emotional reassurance rather than a product spec.

**Reasoning:** Travelers in Tanzania worry about breakdowns in remote areas. "Reliable vehicles" is a feature claim anyone can make. "You'll never worry about being stranded" addresses the actual fear. The internal problem matters more than the external one.

**Tags:** positioning, emotional, brand-strategy
