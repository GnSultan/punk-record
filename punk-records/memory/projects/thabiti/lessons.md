# Thabiti — Lessons Learned

### 2024-04-15 — B2B clients need why-proof before brand work

**Lesson:** Tanzanian B2B clients often start skeptical of 'branding' — need to prove value through why before they engage with the process.

**Context:** Client initially wanted "just a logo." Had to demonstrate through competitor analysis how lack of positioning was costing real customers before they bought into the full brand strategy process.

**Tags:** client-management, b2b, tanzania

---

### 2024-05-01 — Address internal fears, not just external needs

**Lesson:** The most powerful brand positioning addresses the customer's internal fear, not just their external need.

**Context:** "Reliable cars" is an external claim. "You'll never be stranded" addresses the real fear. When we shifted Thabiti's messaging from feature-based to fear-based, the client immediately felt the difference. StoryBrand's internal problem framework proved critical.

**Tags:** brand, positioning, storybrand, emotional
