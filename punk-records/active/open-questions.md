## How to validate AI-generated educational content at scale?

**Context:** ElimuAfrica uses AI for lesson generation. Can't manually review everything. Need systematic quality assurance.

**Hypotheses:**
1. Structured prompts with curriculum context can ensure accuracy (confidence: 60%) — works for simple content, fails for edge cases
2. Teacher spot-checks on samples can catch systemic issues (confidence: 50%) — reduces but doesn't eliminate risk
3. Student outcome data can identify problematic content (confidence: 70%) — feedback loop, but lagging indicator

**Status:** Exploring
**Urgency:** Soon

## What's the right relationship between Apple Empire and ElimuAfrica?

**Context:** Apple Empire provides income. ElimuAfrica is the mission. Both need attention. How do they coexist?

**Hypotheses:**
1. Apple Empire becomes passive income while ElimuAfrica takes focus (confidence: 50%) — requires systematization, may reduce quality
2. Apple Empire content becomes testing ground for ElimuAfrica approaches (confidence: 60%) — storytelling skills transfer, different audiences though
3. Eventual exit from Apple Empire when ElimuAfrica is self-sustaining (confidence: 40%) — long timeline, may lose a valuable feedback channel

**Status:** Open

## Active Tensions

### Cathedral vs. Speed
You prefer seeing the whole architecture before building, but this can delay shipping. When does vision become procrastination? ElimuAfrica is architecturally ambitious. At some point, real students need to use it, even if imperfect.

### Multiple Projects vs. Deep Focus
The Vegapunk identity embraces multiple simultaneous explorations, but depth requires focus. ElimuAfrica, Apple Empire content, Punk Records, client work, conversational AI companion idea — all active. Is this creative abundance or dilution?

### Building Alone vs. Scaling Impact
You hold strategy-design-code together for coherence, but this limits scale. At some point, ElimuAfrica needs to reach more students than one person can build for. The mission requires scale. Scale requires people. People require trust and handoffs. How do you grow without losing the coherence?
